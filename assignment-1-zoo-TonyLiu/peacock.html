Peacock

Peacocks, with their resplendent plumage and enchanting displays, are amongst the most visually stunning birds in the avian kingdom. The male peafowl, known as the peacock, is renowned for its vibrant and iridescent feathers, which fan out into an elaborate display during courtship rituals. Comprising three species, namely the Indian, Green, and Congo peafowl, these birds are native to various regions of Asia and Africa. Beyond their captivating appearance, peafowls also exhibit intriguing social behaviors, residing in flocks and forming intricate hierarchies. With their distinctive calls and striking appearance, peacocks continue to be celebrated symbols of beauty and elegance in cultures around the world, captivating both ornithologists and admirers alike.
